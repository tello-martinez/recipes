Sweet Potato Egg Bowl

Description

Bowl filled with diced sweet potatoes, scrambled eggs, avocados, hemp seeds and more!

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Avocados
  • Hemp Seeds
  • Protein of choice

Steps

  1. cut Sweet Potatos, place in oven at 375 for ~30min
  2. scramble Eggs
  3. cut and slice Avocados
  4. spoon of Hemp Seeds
  5. cook Protein of choice
